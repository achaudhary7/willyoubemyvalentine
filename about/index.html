Our Mission

At WillYouBeMyValentine.fun, our mission is simple: spreading love through technology. We believe that expressing your feelings should be easy, free, and accessible to everyone. Whether you're asking someone to be your valentine or sending a heartfelt e-card, we're here to make it special.

What We Do

We provide free, easy-to-use tools for creating romantic surprises:

  • Free Valentine Link Generator - Create personalized "Will You Be My Valentine?" links with yes/no responses
  • E-Card Creator - Design beautiful digital valentine cards with custom messages and themes
  • Educational Articles - Helpful guides, tips, and ideas for making Valentine's Day special

Our Story

WillYouBeMyValentine.fun was created for Valentine's Day 2026 with a vision to make romantic gestures more accessible and fun. We saw that many people wanted to create personalized valentine experiences but found existing tools complicated or expensive. So we built a platform that's:

  • 100% free - no hidden costs, no signups required
  • Easy to use - create a valentine link in seconds
  • Privacy-focused - minimal data collection, transparent practices
  • Open source - transparent and community-driven
