1. Introduction

Welcome to WillYouBeMyValentine.fun ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage when you visit our website.

By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We provide a free valentine link generator service that allows users to create personalized "Will You Be My Valentine?" links.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Names: When you create a valentine link, you provide names (yours and your valentine's) which are used solely to personalize the link. These names are stored in our secure Cloudflare D1 database to generate the link.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics through Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website and improve our service.
  • Technical Data: We automatically collect certain technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited through Google Analytics.

We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information beyond names used in links.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To generate and display personalized valentine links
  • To track link views and responses (Yes/No clicks) through our secure API
  • To analyze website usage and improve our service through Google Analytics
  • To improve user experience and site functionality
  • To ensure website security and prevent abuse

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties except as described in this policy.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and store certain information. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.

Google Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics (Measurement ID: G-HDVZBTE0GC) to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information such as:

  • How often users visit our website
  • What pages they visit
  • How long they stay on pages
  • What website they came from

This information is aggregated and anonymous. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cloudflare

We use Cloudflare Workers and D1 database to store and retrieve link data. Cloudflare does not use cookies for our tracking service.

5. Third-Party Services

We use several third-party services to provide our website functionality:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data to help us understand how visitors use our site.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts (Poppins and Dancing Script) to display text on our website. When you visit our site, Google may collect information about your use of fonts, including your IP address.

For more information, see Google Fonts FAQ.

Cloudflare Workers & D1

We use Cloudflare Workers and D1 database to store link data securely. This allows us to generate and track valentine links without loading any third-party SDKs in your browser.

For more information, see Cloudflare Privacy Policy.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • We use HTTPS encryption to secure data transmission
  • We store minimal data and only for as long as necessary
  • We use Cloudflare's secure global infrastructure for data storage
  • We do not store sensitive personal information

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for users of all ages, including children. However, we are committed to protecting children's privacy online:

  • We collect minimal information (only names used in links)
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Parents can contact us to review or delete their child's information

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can request information about what data we have collected
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your data (names used in links)

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Us section below.
